How Dreamtech Boreholes supports you

We help homeowners, businesses and farms secure lasting water supply through expert detection, professional drilling, premium installations and ongoing support — delivered nationwide across South Africa.

Water Detection

Before any drill touches the ground, our team uses geophysical surveying to accurately locate underground water — giving you confidence in yield before you commit to a project.

Professional Drilling

Heavy-duty rigs, premium steel casing and a skilled crew ensure every borehole is engineered to match your site's geology — built to last, wherever you are in South Africa.

Pumps & Filtration

From premium submersible pumps sized to your borehole's depth and output to multi-stage filtration and UV sterilization, we deliver clean, reliable water from the ground to your tap.

Testing & Maintenance

Comprehensive yield testing and water quality lab checks confirm your supply is safe and sustainable, while ongoing maintenance keeps your system running at its best year after year.
